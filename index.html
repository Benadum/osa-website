Who We Are

Ong'icha Soccer Academy (OSA) is a grassroots youth empowerment organization based at Ong'icha Primary/Secondary School along the Kisii-Oyugis Highway.

Founded by Zachary Obiero, we support orphans and vulnerable children by providing access to football, healthcare, and practical life skills.

Facebook LinkedIn Instagram

Our Pillars of Impact

Football

Training boys & girls (OSA Kenya Team), participating in local tournaments, and partner partnerships with Save The Ball & UEFA.

Healthcare

Managing menstruation periods for girls, sanitary towel distribution, and general health awareness workshops.

Agribusiness

Poultry farming and Kitchen gardens to teach self-sustainability, economic independence, and food security.

Community

Practice life skills, mentorship, and building a stronger Ong'icha community through volunteerism.
